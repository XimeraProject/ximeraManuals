In this setting, the file \verb!worksheetCollection.tex! might look something
like
\begin{verbatim}
\document{xourse}

\maketitle
\begin{document}
\activity{someWorksheet/someWorksheet}
\activity{anotherWorksheet/anotherWorkSheet}
\end{document}
\end{verbatim}
